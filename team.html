Our Team

We are a creative team of young software engineers. Our team has a wide range of experience and talents. This project is our senior capstone project in the Northern Arizona University CS degree program, under direction of Dr. James Palmer. This project has been done in conjunction with Trent Hare of the USGS in Flagstaff, Arizona.

USGS Sponsor

Team Leader – Eric Ghazal

Duties – Include but not limited to organizing meetings, preparing timetables and due dates, progression plans, recording.

Communicator/Webmaster – Jason Hedlund

Duties – Responsible for facilitating communications between the project contact and the project’s faculty mentor. Will also handle communications regarding team meeting times and project deadlines. Communicator is also responsible for taking the minutes, and making sure the team gets all communications through email. Also responsible for all website maintenance for the team’s website. All change requests to the website are executed and done by the Webmaster.

Architect – Andrew Carter

Duties – Responsible for ensuring that core architectural decisions are followed during implementation. This includes additional responsibilities, like updating the requirements and design documents, and determining what type of architectures and design patterns to use. This information will be stored on the team website.

Lead Programmer – Terrence Luther

Duties - Responsible for organizing tasks to be done pertaining to development. Reviewing code submissions and leading coding efforts for the project.
